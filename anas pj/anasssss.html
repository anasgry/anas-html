    About Messi

    Lionel Andrés Messi, born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, is considered one of the greatest football players of all time. Known for his incredible dribbling, vision, passing, and finishing, Messi has left an indelible mark on the sport.

    He started his professional career with FC Barcelona, where he spent over 20 years, winning numerous La Liga titles, Champions League trophies, and individual awards including multiple Ballon d'Or titles. Messi later joined Paris Saint-Germain and eventually Inter Miami.

    Career Highlights

    • 7× Ballon d'Or Winner
    • 4× Champions League Titles
    • 10× La Liga Championships
    • Copa America Champion (2021)
    • FIFA World Cup Winner (2022)
    • All-time top scorer for Barcelona
